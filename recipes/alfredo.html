Alfredo

This is a delicious, creamy alfredo that I have made for myself and my brother. It's a simple recipe that can be done by anyone.

Cooking this up with my little brother is a way that I get to bond with him. Watching him enjoy this meal is more than I can ask for.

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Garlic
  • Fettucini Pasta
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Throw garlic and butter into pan
  3. Into the pan, add heavy whipping cream and cheese
  4. Add pasta into the sauce
